Nottingham Eastern Relief Road

Construction Project Management Simulation

You are the Project Manager responsible for constructing a new 4km dual carriageway, including 3 bridges (Railway Bridge, Shelford Road Bridge, River Trent Bridge), earthworks, drainage and carriageway layers.

The simulation runs in decision periods. Each period you allocate resources, and the simulation applies real-world effects: weather, equipment breakdowns, and productivity variation.

  • Contract value: £18,500,000 (to be earned)
  • Contract duration: 400 working days
  • Liquidated damages: £5,000/day if late
  • Project start: 1 March 2025

This simulation is based on the Dam Construction Game (V7.1) methodology, adapted for UK highway construction.
